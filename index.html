Stat glossary

OPS
On-base percentage plus slugging. A quick read on reaching base and power.
fWAR
FanGraphs wins above replacement. A broad estimate of total player value.
wRC+
Weighted runs created plus. Shown on player pages when available from the local FanGraphs snapshot.
ISO
Isolated power. Slugging minus batting average.
